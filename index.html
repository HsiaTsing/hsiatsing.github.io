I'm now an ML/CV researcher at SenseTime Research (Beijing, China). I received my Ph.D. degree from the State Key Laboratory of Virtual Reality Technology and Systems (虚拟现实技术与系统国家重点实验室) at Beihang University under the supervision of Prof. Aimin Hao (郝爱民 教授) from Beihang University and Prof. Hong Qin (秦洪 教授) from Stony Brook University. My major interests are in computer graphics, computer vision and machine learning, more specifically in digital geometry processing and medical image analysis. I'm with great passion on exploring algorithms to impove human health.

Here's my cv in English and Chinese.
