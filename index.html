Qing Xia （夏 清）

I'm now an ML/CV research scientist at SenseTime Research (Beijing, China) and leading a R&D team that focus on developing cutting-edge algorithms and products for medical use. Meanwhile, I'm also a post-doctoral researcher at the iMoon: Intelligent Media and Cognition Lab, Tsinghua University, jointly supervised by Prof. Yue Gao (Tsinghua University) and Prof. Xiaogang Wang (The Chinese University of Hong Kong). Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree from the State Key Laboratory of Virtual Reality Technology and Systems at Beihang University under the joint supervision of Prof. Aimin Hao (Beihang University) and Prof. Hong Qin (Stony Brook University). My major interests are in computer graphics, computer vision and machine learning, more specifically in digital geometry processing and medical image analysis.

Here is my cv in English and Chinese, and you can also find me on Google Scholar, Research Gate, and Linkedin.
